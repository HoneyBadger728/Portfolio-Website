About Me

Hello! I’m Blake Davis, a passionate and adaptable full-stack developer with a unique blend of experience in education and technology. I hold a Bachelor’s Degree from Florida Gulf Coast University and spent six fulfilling years as a high school educator. Teaching honed my ability to break down complex problems, communicate effectively, and lead teams toward success. My journey into tech began at an educational software startup, where I contributed as a front-end developer. This experience deepened my interest in creating intuitive, user-centered applications and inspired me to expand my expertise. Through dedicated learning on Codecademy, I’ve transitioned into a full-stack developer, eager to craft innovative solutions that bridge the gap between people and technology. When I’m not coding, I enjoy exploring emerging technologies, mentoring others, and finding creative ways to make a positive impact. Let’s work together to build something extraordinary!
